Latent Diffusion Model

From-scratch Latent Denoising Diffusion in PyTorch

● Built from scratch on ImageNet-100

What it does

  • Built a Latent Diffusion Model entirely from scratch in PyTorch.
  • Used a Variational Autoencoder to compress images into a latent space for the ImageNet One Hundred dataset.
  • Implemented both a Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Model schedule and a Denoising Diffusion Implicit Model schedule.
  • Produced high quality image synthesis with the probabilistic schedule and faster inference with the implicit schedule.
  • Structured the training pipeline so that latent space compression and the diffusion process were cleanly separated and reusable.
